Chocolate Fudge Cake

Description

Chocolate Fudge Cake is a rich and decadent dessert that features a moist and dense chocolate cake layered with a smooth and creamy chocolate fudge frosting. The cake is made with high-quality cocoa powder, butter, sugar, eggs, and flour, and it's typically baked in a round cake pan. Once the cake is cooled, a layer of chocolate fudge frosting is spread over the top, and the process is repeated for additional layers if desired. The frosting is made with chocolate chips, butter, heavy cream, and powdered sugar, and it's heated and mixed until smooth and glossy. Chocolate Fudge Cake is a beloved dessert that is perfect for special occasions or anytime you're in the mood for a rich and indulgent treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Bake a box chocolate cake as directed. While hot from the oven, poke holes over entire cake with a fork. While the cake is cooking prepare the fudge sauce.
  2. For the fudge sauce: In a saucepan, combine butter, sugar, cocoa and cream. Stir over medium heat until a full boil starts. Cook at a full boil for 2 minutes. Stir in vanilla. Pour while hot over warm cake. Sprinkle with chopped walnuts. Serve alone or with ice-cream.
